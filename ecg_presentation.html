The Solution

ECG Interpreter — What It Does

Paper ECG Friendly

  • Accepts smartphone photos of printed ECGs
  • AI digitiser converts image → waveform
  • Works with any 12-lead machine

Fully Offline

  • Runs on a laptop or tablet
  • No internet or cloud subscription needed
  • ~121 MB total model weight

Clinical Decision Support

  • Flags HIGH / MOD / LOW risk in <30 s
  • Evidence-weighted differential diagnoses
  • Augments — does not replace — the clinician
Real scenario: PHC nurse in Kano photographs ECG strip → AI flags Reduced EF + Pulmonary HTN (HIGH risk) → Urgent referral issued. Cost: ₦4,800 ECG + a smartphone photo.[8]

Diagnosis Engine — How the AI Reasons

3-Source Evidence Fusion

PTB-XL Model Probabilities (ECG pattern recognition)
+
Morphological Features (ST segments, Q waves, rhythm)
+
EchoNext Structural Probabilities
↓
Evidence-Weighted Scoring Engine
↓
Ranked Differential Diagnoses + Confidence

Evidence Logic

  • Anchors — required findings to consider a diagnosis
  • Supporters — boost confidence by +10–25%
  • Against — dampen score (e.g. narrow QRS → not LBBB)
confidence = (anchor + support_bonus) × dampen_factor

Limitations & Roadmap

Honest Assessment & Next Steps

Population Gap

Trained on European & US data. Nigerian-specific morphology (RHD, African cardiomyopathy) not yet represented[2]

→ Fine-tuning study planned with LUTH / UCH dataset

Image Quality

Digitiser requires clear, landscape ECG photos (min 800×600 px)

→ In-app quality guidance before inference

Clinical Reminder

AI output is decision support only — licensed clinician must confirm before treatment
